## 📊 Case Studies

### 1. Urban Mobility Demand Forecasting (Citi Bike, NYC)
- **Objective:** Identify key drivers of bike demand to support operational planning  
- **Methods:** EDA, correlation analysis, OLS regression, LASSO, tree-based models  
- **Insight:** Demand is strongly influenced by **seasonality, weather, and time of day**

---

### 2. Predictive Maintenance (Manufacturing)
- **Objective:** Predict faulty parts using production data  
- **Methods:** Classification models + performance evaluation (accuracy, precision, recall)  
- **Insight:** Failures driven primarily by **process variables (torque, wear, temperature)**  
- **Outcome:** Random Forest provided the best performance

---

### 3. Auction Strategy Simulation
- **Objective:** Compare procurement costs across auction formats  
- **Methods:** Monte Carlo simulation of first-price and second-price auctions  
- **Insight:** Demonstrates pricing differences and validates auction theory concepts
